💰 Additional Revenue Target — Forward Booking Rate Reduction Opportunity
This screen shows already-confirmed 2026 bookings grouped by guest arrival month. The concept is simple: if Trip Factory negotiates even a small reduction on rates already booked, every dollar saved flows straight to the bottom line as additional commission or margin.

How to read this: Room-Nights = total rooms × total nights per booking, summed across all bookings in that month — this is the industry unit for rate calculations. ADR = Average Daily Rate = Revenue ÷ Room-Nights (what ORN pays per room per night on average). Rev/Stay-Night = Revenue ÷ Stay-Nights (avg total spend per night, across all rooms in a booking — useful for package pricing).

Additional Revenue Formula: Rate Reduction ($) × Total Room-Nights = Additional Revenue. Adjust the slider below to see the instant impact of any rate reduction.
